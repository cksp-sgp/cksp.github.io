Wooden Fire Rated & Non-Fire Rated Doors.

Our wooden door solutions not only provide resistance against fires, but are also available in a variety of finishes, encompassing the definition of form follows function. With the wide variety of customizable options available, our wooden doors are ready to be tailored to meet your specific needs, while adhering to SS 332: 2018 (Singapore Standard Specification For Fire Doors).

  • In compliance with SS 332: 2018
  • Variety of finishes available
  • Fire rating ranging from ½ hour – 2 hours
  • Customizable to a size that fits your requirements
  • Vision panel option available
